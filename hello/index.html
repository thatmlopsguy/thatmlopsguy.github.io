👋 Hello

This is my Hello page, a concept popularized by Alastar Johnson.

You can find out a bit more on the about page. My now page lists what I’m focused on currently.

Here is how I prefer to keep in touch, and why:

  • My favorite way to converse is email 📧. See contact page.
  • For a quick chat you can talk add me on Signal. Again see contact page.
  • My least favorite means of communication is via phone call.
  • I don’t use any Meta products: Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp or Threads.
  • I don’t use Twitter (nor what it has morphed into) or LinkedIn.

And that is it for me. I’m always looking for new people to connect with, learn from and collaborate with, so get in touch without hesitation.
